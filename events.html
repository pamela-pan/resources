× TAKEAWAYS
  • What does the graph-maker want you to feel about level, trend, and/or volatility?
  • Is the data censored on the left or on the right?
  • Are there missing data points in between?
  • Does the range on the vertical axis make sense?
  • Is the zero set at 0?
  • Are you being shown comparison data for no apparent reason?
  • Is the aspect ratio strange?
  • Does the graph use labels and fonts to suggest causal relationships or to promote emotional responses?
